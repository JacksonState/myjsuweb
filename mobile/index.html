The NEW Mobile App of Jackson State University

myJSU Mobile

Staying Connected with Jackson State University(JSU) just got easier with the new MyJSU app for IOS and Android devices. This free mobile app puts JSU at your fingertips while you are on the go.

Download the mobile application to enjoy all the new features:

  • Events - Browse the events calendar to find upcoming events on campus.
  • News - Read the latest news stories.
  • Directory - Faculty, staff and departmental directory at your fingertips.
  • Athletics - See game scores, watch videos and much more.
  • Maps - GPS enabled campus map, location where you are on campus and find where you are going with turn-by-turn direction from any building on campus.
  • Students - Sign-in to view your class schedules, grades, alerts and more.
  • Course Catalog - Access class schedules, times, locations, buildings and instructors.
  • Important Numbers - View relevant telephone numbers at your fingertips.
  • Multimedia - stay in touch by listening to WJSU 88.5 and watching JSU videos on your mobile devices.
  • Alumni - Stay connected anywhere, anytime and everywhere.

Go to your Apple App Store and your Google play Store and search Jackson State to download the NEW app TODAY!
